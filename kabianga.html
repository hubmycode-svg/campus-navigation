Academic Schools

Five schools, one vision

University of Kabianga offers programmes across five schools with 15 departments, combining rigorous academics with practical, community-focused learning.

School of Agriculture & Biotechnology

Specializes in crop science, animal science, and applied biotechnology for sustainable agriculture in Kenya's highlands. Home to research farms and greenhouses.

Crop Science Animal Science Agricultural Economics Biotechnology
School of Science & Technology

Covers computing, mathematics, physics, and applied sciences with modern ICT laboratories and a strong emphasis on research and innovation.

Computer Science Mathematics Physics & Electronics Chemistry
School of Business & Economics

Trains future business leaders, accountants, and economists for East Africa's growing economy through case-based learning and industry partnerships.

Business Administration Accounting & Finance Economics Marketing
School of Education & Social Sciences

Prepares educators, sociologists, and development workers for Kenya's communities, with teacher training programmes recognised by the TSC.

Education Sociology Psychology Communication
School of Natural Resources & Environmental Management

Focuses on ecology, forestry, and environmental conservation, benefiting from the rich Kericho ecosystem including the nearby tea-growing highlands and forests.

Forestry Wildlife Management Environmental Science Geography
Our Campuses

Three campuses across Kericho County

UoK serves students from across the South Rift through its Main Campus and two satellite campuses.

Main Campus — Kabianga

Located in the scenic Kabianga complex within Kericho County (0°26'45"S, 35°08'17"E), the main campus is UoK's beating heart. It sits alongside Kabianga Boys High School and the historic Kabianga Tea Farm in the lush tea-growing highlands.

All five schools, the main library, administration offices, ICT centre, student hostels, the health clinic, and sports facilities are found here.

Kapkatet Campus

Kapkatet satellite campus serves students in the eastern parts of Kericho County. It offers selected undergraduate programmes and is closely linked to the main campus for academic coordination.

The campus was personally addressed by the Vice-Chancellor and continues to expand its programme offerings for students in the Bureti sub-county area.

Kericho Town Campus

Situated in Kericho's central business district, the town campus makes higher education accessible for working professionals and part-time learners throughout the region.

This campus primarily runs evening and weekend programmes, serving the needs of professionals seeking to advance their qualifications without disrupting their careers.

A center of innovation and excellence

University of Kabianga is a fully-fledged, ISO 9001:2015 certified public university located in the lush tea-growing highlands of Kericho County, Kenya. From humble roots as an agricultural training centre in 1925, UoK was awarded university charter in 2013 and now serves over 7,000 students.

With a motto of "Innovation and Excellence", UoK combines rigorous academic training with hands-on research and community service across five schools and three campuses.
