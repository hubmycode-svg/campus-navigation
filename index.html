Academic Schools

Five Schools, One University

UoK offers diverse academic programmes across five major schools.

School of Agriculture & Biotechnology

Focused on crop science, animal science, and biotechnology research.

School of Science & Technology

Covers computing, physics, mathematics and ICT innovation.

School of Business & Economics

Business, finance, accounting, and entrepreneurship programmes.

School of Education & Social Sciences

Teacher training, sociology, psychology, and communication studies.

School of Natural Resources & Environmental Management

Environmental science, forestry, and conservation studies.

Our Campuses

Three Campuses Across Kericho County

UoK operates Main Campus, Kapkatet Campus, and Kericho Town Campus.

Main Campus — Kabianga

The main campus hosts all schools, administration, ICT centre, library, hostels and sports facilities.

Kapkatet Campus

Satellite campus serving eastern Kericho with selected academic programmes.

Kericho Town Campus

Evening and weekend programmes for working students and professionals.

A Center of Innovation and Excellence

University of Kabianga is a public university located in Kericho County, Kenya, known for agriculture, science, and innovation-driven education.

Established in 2013 as a chartered university, it serves thousands of students across five schools and three campuses.

1925 Founded as agricultural training centre
2013 Granted university charter
Today Over 7,000 students enrolled
